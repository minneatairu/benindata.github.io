This platform showcases an image dataset of Benin Bronzes compiled by Minne. All images were manually cut out from auction catalogs and journals published between 1900-1965.

Each image includes a front and back view as they appear in the original publications. To view the back, please hover over the respective image.

The dataset categories listed are based on my artistic goals for my Igun AI project, and therefore, do not represent culturally relevant categorizations in Benin.
